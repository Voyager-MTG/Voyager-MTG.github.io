### Ajha, the Wish for Home
<change-preview>
https://github.com/Voyager-MTG/Voyager-MTG.github.io/blob/0612b44bbeb0bec678a8a44dc749c993895182d4/sets/WAW-files/img/9_Ajha,%20the%20Wish%20for%20Home.jpg?raw=true
https://github.com/Voyager-MTG/Voyager-MTG.github.io/blob/f09e2b68b6d3d176f239e79056fb383c047aac63/sets/WAW-files/img/9_Ajha,%20the%20Wish%20for%20Home.jpg?raw=true
</change-preview>

Ajha provides a 6 mana body and up to 5 mana value worth of creatures easily at base. The fact that this provides much more mana worth of stats spread across multiple bodies and that it has synergies with cards such as ploy cards or eye of the abominable makes this suspect. It additionally has synergies with other things (like ploy cards again) through its emerge mode. Overall, we found that the amount of mana this card costs does not align with its effect.

</left-card>

### Wishtouched Outcast
<change-preview>
https://github.com/Voyager-MTG/Voyager-MTG.github.io/blob/0612b44bbeb0bec678a8a44dc749c993895182d4/sets/WAW-files/img/109_Wishtouched%20Outcast.jpg?raw=true
https://github.com/Voyager-MTG/Voyager-MTG.github.io/blob/f09e2b68b6d3d176f239e79056fb383c047aac63/sets/WAW-files/img/109_Wishtouched%20Outcast.jpg?raw=true
</change-preview>

Wishtouched Outcast avoids multiple of Dark Confidant's main downsides. For one, it picks the card, so you are at effectively no risk of losing lots of life as you are with confidant. Additionally, outcast also recoups the life you lose from it via its lifelink. We deemed that Wishtouched Outcast draws too many cards and avoids the downsides usually associated with this kind of effect and think this warrants a change.

</right-card>
